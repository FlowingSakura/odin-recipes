Miso Soup

What is Miso Soup?

Miso Soup: Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup made from miso paste, which is a fermented soybean paste. It's a simple yet comforting soup that's often served as a side dish with meals. Here's a simple recipe for miso soup:

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 block of silken tofu, cubed
  • 2 green onions, chopped

    • Instructions:

  • In a medium pot, bring the dashi to a simmer.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together the miso paste with a small amount of the hot dashi until smooth.
  • Add the miso mixture to the pot and stir to combine.
  • Add the cubed tofu to the pot and simmer for a few minutes.
  • Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
