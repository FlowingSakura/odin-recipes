Matcha Ice Cream

What is Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream?

Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream: Matcha is a finely ground powder of green tea leaves that's commonly used in Japanese cuisine. Matcha green tea ice cream is a delicious and refreshing dessert that's easy to make at home. Here's a simple recipe for matcha green tea ice cream:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon matcha powder
  • 6 large egg yolks

    • Instructions:

  • In a medium saucepan, heat the cream, milk, sugar, and matcha powder over medium heat, stirring until the sugar has dissolved.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until smooth.
  • Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly.
  • Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 8-10 minutes.
